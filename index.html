presto

A digital shopping cart like no other

Keep everything in one place

Presto is a digital shopping cart with a simple interface to help you organize and keep track of your items while you're in store.

Know before you go

Enter items manually or swipe right to scan an item's price tag and add it to your digital cart. Presto will auto-magically show you the price of the item with tax before you get to the checkout.

Budget like a pro

Presto also keeps an accurate total of your cart's price as you go, so you can stick to your budget.
